📖 How to set up your Google Sheet
  1. Open Google Sheets and create a new spreadsheet
  2. Add columns: Ref | PIN | Name | WhatsApp | School | Grade | Pack | Qty | Payment | Date | Total | Status | Notes | Timestamp
  3. Go to File → Share → Publish to web
  4. Select "Entire Document" and "Web page"
  5. Click Publish and copy the URL
  6. Paste the URL in the box above and click LOAD

💡 Pro tip: Use Zapier or Make.com to auto-populate your Sheet whenever a new order is placed on your order page.
